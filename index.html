My work lives at the overlap of rigorous research, clear strategic thinking, and considered craft. I have a particular fondness for the 0→1 problem — the moment before the product exists.

I believe good design is argued for, not just executed. The best outcomes I have been part of came from designers who could speak business and researchers who could speak design.

I like clean information design, lemon cheesecakes and stormy views.
